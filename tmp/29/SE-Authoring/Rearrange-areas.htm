Rearrange areas

You can rearrange the content of your financial statements document by moving areas.

To move an area:

  1. Go to the Document Map on the left side of the page.

  2. Locate the area that you want to move and select the Move icon ().

  3. Drag the area then drop it in the desired location on the map.

A grid displays in the map to mark the borders of each area. Use the grid for guidance as you drag and drop.
