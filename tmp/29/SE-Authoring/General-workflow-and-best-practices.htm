General workflow and best practices

Your first step is to request a product, then enable the desired features in your product. Once you're done with that, you're ready to start authoring your product template.

You can use the following workflow to author your product template.

  1. Create your template

  2. Verify and customize the product settings

  3. Define groupings

  4. Define the engagement glossary terms

  5. Add checklists

  6. Add queries

  7. Add letters or memos

  8. Set up the financial statements document

  9. Set up the risk, control and risk assessment reports

  10. Set up materiality and summary of misstatements documents

  11. Develop custom forms

  12. Finalize the draft and distribute the template

Best practices

For a smooth authoring experience, we recommend that you:

  • use Google Chrome

  • create a standardized design documents for content authoring

  • backup your solution drafts frequently

  • communicate between team members on saving and deleting drafts
