Analytics.AI test results

This feature is only available with CaseWare Analytics.AI, part of the CaseWare Cloud suite.

Learn more about Cloud.

The Results page gives you an overview of the engagement’s transactions using four different widgets.

Analytics.AI ranks the transactions in terms of overall risk in the Risk Summary widget.

The High Risk Exceptions widget shows the top four tests that contributed the highest dollar value to risky transactions.

High Risk Users shows the top five users that posted to risky transactions.

High Risk Journals shows the top five journals with the riskiest transactions.

Elements inside the widgets are clickable so that you can quickly filter on areas of interest. For example, you can click on High Score in the Risk Summary to isolate the riskiest transactions.

You can also scroll down the page to see a list of transactions, in descending order of overall risk.

Click on a transaction to see more details.

Click on a test button, for example Two Digit Benford’s Law, to highlight areas of concern. A brief description of the test also displays.

Left panel

Click on any of the options in the left panel to quickly filter information shown in the widgets. A filter tag also appears to show your selection.

Widgets

Click on the information in any of the widgets to display more information. The information shown in the widgets changes to reflect your selection. A filter tag also appears to show your selection.
