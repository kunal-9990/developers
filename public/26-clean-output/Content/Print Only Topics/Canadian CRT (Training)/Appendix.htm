Appendix

Functionality currently not supported by Review and Compilation – Canada

Provincial jurisdictions

Review and Compilation – Canada currently does not support filings for the following provinces:

  • Alberta (expected May 2018)

  • Quebec (expected by end of 2018)

CRA filing functionality

Review and Compilation – Canada currently does not support Corporation Internet Filing for the following corporation types:

  • Income Tax Regulation 403 - Insurance corporations

  • Income Tax Regulation 404 - Banks

  • Income Tax Regulation 405 - Trust and loan corporations

  • Income Tax Regulation 412 - Divided businesses

Additionally, Review and Compilation – Canada does not currently support the CRA's Attach-a-doc E-file service.
