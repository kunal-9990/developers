What is Review and Compilation – Canada?

CaseWareCloud Review and Compilation – Canada is a comprehensive, review, compilation and tax engagement solution for small- and mid-sized firms, featuring guided client onboarding and continuous optimization.

Review and Compilation – Canada will guide you through compilations, reviews. and tax engagements. As you set up a client file, one of the first questions you will be asked is the type of engagement. The software will automatically populate the client file with the checklists needed to complete the engagement based upon the answers to engagement set up questions. The accountant or auditor has the ability to further modify the checklists adding additional steps if needed. Each of the engagement profiles includes procedures to meet the requirements of the relevant regulatory standards.

Note: This beta release of Review and Compilation – Canada limits some functionality.

About this guide

The practice aids in this guide have been designed for a small and medium-sized (SME) non-public company, and do not address the wide range of circumstances or specific industry issues that may exist for a particular client or groups of clients (such as those operating in a specialized industry, government or non-profit organization, complex IT environments, etc.). Consequently, the forms should always be tailored to address the known facts and circumstances of each engagement.
