What is OnPoint PCR?

CaseWareCloud OnPoint is a comprehensive, cloud-based audit engagement solution for small- and mid-sized firms, featuring guided client onboarding and continuous optimization using the latest methodology from the AICPA, and offering real-time collaboration with your colleagues and clients. It combines CaseWare’s cloud platform, AICPA guidance, and distribution from CPA.com.

 

CaseWareCloud OnPoint PCR will guide you through preparations, compilations and reviews. As you set up a client file, the first question you will be asked is the type of engagement (profile). The software will automatically populate the client file with the checklists needed to complete the engagement based upon the answers to engagement set up questions. The accountant always has the ability to further modify the checklists adding additional steps if needed. Each of the engagement profiles (described below) includes procedures to meet the requirements of AR­C 60 General Principles for Engagements Performed in Accordance with SSARS.

Primary Feature: Financial Statement Reference Copy creation assistance

What it is:

  • Generates reference copies of your client’s financial statements

  • Automatically imports information and abstracts into a financial statement format

  • Optimizes your effort in assisting with your clients’ financial statement creation.

Benefit:

  • Reduce time spent manually referencing financial statement inputs with our in-line reference financial statement that creates digital links between a financial statement and relevant client data.

  • Included financial statement analytics tools that are driven directly from financial statement entries.

Feature 2: Guided Client Collaboration

What it is:

  • Client collaboration prompts as needed during an engagement

  • Direct client query to “binder” links

  • Direct import of financial data from top packages like QuickBooks, Xero, and Excel

Benefit:

  • Quickly generate accurate engagement letters for your clients using our 4-step guided onboarding tool.

  • Simplifies client collaboration by monitoring your engagement progression and providing prompts whenever communication with your client is needed, all of which is handled from within the system.

  • Eliminates import and translation steps by inserting client financial trial balances into the correct audit or review documentation

The practice aids have been designed for a small and medium-sized (SME) non-public company, and do not address the wide range of circumstances or specific industry issues that may exist for a particular client or groups of clients (such as those operating in a specialized industry, government or non-profit organization, complex IT environments, etc.). Consequently, the forms should always be tailored to address the known facts and circumstances of each engagement.
