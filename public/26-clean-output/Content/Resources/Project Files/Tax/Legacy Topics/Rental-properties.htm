Working with Rental Properties

  • Completing the Rental Income Workchart

Completing the Rental Income Workchart

According to the CRA, you can claim CCA on rental property when its available for use. For more information on Rental income, refer to the Rental Income 2015 CRA guide.

Procedures

  1. In the Workchart section, open the Rental Income Workchart per Property workchart.

  2. In the Property Profile section, fill in the property details and select between the following three options:

  • Fully owned by the tax payer

  • Partnership

  • Co-ownership

Option Description
Ownership of property Select this option if the property is fully-owned.
Partnership

Name of partnership

Enter the partnership name if the building or certain leasehold interests is owned by partnership and principally used to generate gross revenue from rent.

Corporation's percentage of partnership of the property

Enter the corporation's percentage of partnership from the T5013- Statement of Partnership Income slip.

T5013

Enter the net income amount from the T5013- Statement of Partnership Income slip.

Other co-ownership If the property is co-owned, enter the co-ownership percentage for the property.

  1. If the property is fully-owned or co-owned, enter the total gross rental income in the income section. Click to add more lines.

  2. In the Expenses section, enter the non-corporation's percentage. In the Total Expense column, enter the expense amount.

    3. Notes: 

    • The Non-Corporate Portion column will automatically be calculated based on the non-corporation portion percentage except for Advertising.

    • You need to remove the non-corporation portion percentage to override the non-corporate portion for expenses except Advertising.

  3. Fill in the CCA details in the CCA workchart. Please see CCA and Fixed assets for more information.

  4. In Area F, enter the total of all amounts you received or will receive for disposing of land in the fiscal period.

Viewing Rental Income Summaries

You can view a detail or a condensed rental income summary for a period of 5 years.

In the Workchart section, open the Rental Income 5 Year Summary for each Property workchart to view a detailed summary of rental income for each property over 5 years.

Open the Rental Workchart - Total to view a condensed summary of rental income for each property over 5 years.
