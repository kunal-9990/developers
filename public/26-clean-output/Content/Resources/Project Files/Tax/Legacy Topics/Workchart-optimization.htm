Completing the Optimization Workchart

In arriving at taxable income, there are a few deductions that can be claimed at the discretion of the taxpayer. For example, CCA is a discretionary deduction which you can defer to be used in a future tax year.

In this section you will learn how to,

  • Optimize to maximize or not claim discretionary deductions

  • Optimize to apply discretionary deductions for zero taxable income

  • Optimize to get a targeted taxable income

 

Optimize to maximize or not claim discretionary deductions

  1. In the Tax Planning section, open the Optimization Workchart .

  2. In the Table of discretionary deductions, it will display all the deductions that a user can claim for their tax year.

  3. Select 1. Maximize all discretionary deductions, even creating or increasing taxable loss to claim all listed deductions or select 4. Do not claim any discretionary deductions.
