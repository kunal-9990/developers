View material groups and accounts in the trial balance

This feature is only available with products on the CaseWareCloud SE platform.

Learn more about Smart Engagement products.

Once the materiality form is complete, you can easily review material accounts or groups in the trial balance.

To view material groups and accounts in the trial balance:

  1. From the top menu, select Trial Balance.

  2. Select the grouping menu and choose one of the available grouping structures.

  3. Select the Settings icon (), then select the Materiality indicator check box to enable the column.

Any groups or accounts that are material now are now indicated with a green icon.
