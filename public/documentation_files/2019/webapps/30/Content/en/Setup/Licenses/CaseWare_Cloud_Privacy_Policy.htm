  10. Where Your Personal Information is Stored

    1. CWC uses third party data hosting providers (Amazon Web Services) to host its Cloud Services and Subscriber Data on servers located in throughout the world.

    2. Upon subscribing to the CaseWare Cloud Services, CWC shall inform you of the jurisdiction in which the server that has be allocated to host your Subscriber Data and Personal Information is located. At present, CWC uses servers in the United States, Australia, Canada and Europe. You may consent to such allocation, or refuse a server so allocated. If you refuse allocation of a server, CWC may not be able to provide you with CaseWare Cloud Services.

    3. For subscribers to the CaseWare Cloud Services in the United States, CWC will attempt to allocate a server in the United States. For subscribers to the CaseWare Cloud Services in Europe, CWC will attempt to allocate a server in Europe. For subscribers of CaseWare Services in Canada, CWC will attempt to allocate to a server in Canada. For subscribers to the CaseWare Cloud Services in Australia, CWC will attempt to allocate a server in Australia.

    4. By entering personal information into the CaseWare Cloud Services, you consent to that Personal Information being hosted on servers where allocated. Each data hosting provider’s role is limited to providing a hosting and storage service to CWC, and CWC has taken reasonable measure to ensure that our data hosting providers will not access the data, and will use the necessary level of protection for, Personal Information. They do not control, and are not permitted to access or use your personal information, except for the limited purpose of storing the information.

    5. If You do not want Your Personal Information to be transferred to a server as described above, You should not provide CWC with Personal Information or use the CaseWare Cloud Service.

  11. Safeguards

    1. CWC is committed to protecting the security of your Personal Information and we take all reasonable precautions to protect it from unauthorized access, modification and disclosure. Your Personal Information is stored on secure servers that are encrypted, and all data transferred between you and the CaseWare Cloud Services is encrypted.

    2. However, the Internet is not in itself a secure environment and CWC cannot give an absolute assurance that your Personal Information will be secure at all times. Transmission of Personal Information over the Internet is at your own risk and you should only enter, or instruct the entering of, Personal Information to the CaseWare Cloud Services within a secure environment.

    3. CWC will advise you at the first reasonable opportunity upon discovering or being advised of an incident where your Personal Information is lost, stolen, accessed, used, disclosed, copied, modified or disposed of by unauthorized persons or in an unauthorized manner.

  12. Individual Access
