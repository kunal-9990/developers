Fusion RCT - Review, Compilation and Tax

Fusion RCT is a comprehensive Review, Compilation and Tax engagement solution for small- and mid-sized firms, featuring guided client onboarding and continuous optimization.

Fusion RCT will guide you through compilations, reviews. and tax engagements. As you set up a client file, one of the first questions you will be asked is the type of engagement. The software will automatically populate the client file with the checklists needed to complete the engagement based upon the answers to engagement set up questions. The accountant or auditor has the ability to further modify the checklists adding additional steps if needed. Each of the engagement profiles includes procedures to meet the requirements of the relevant regulatory standards.

Fusion RCT's content and workflows follow the CPA's PEG guidelines. For more information about PEG, download the PDFs below.
