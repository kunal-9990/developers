Upload trial balance in Analytics.AI

This feature is only available with CaseWare Analytics.AI, part of the CaseWare Cloud suite.

Learn more about Cloud.

You must upload a trial balance for your engagement to reconcile your data on the Data Completeness page, and to be able to use Influencers.

To upload the trial balance:

  1. Select 0-3 Upload Trial Balance from the Documents tab.
    The Trial Balance page displays. Available options will vary depending on how your product is set up.

  2. Use one of the available options to import the trial balance.
