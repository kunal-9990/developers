CaseWareCloud Analytics

CaseWareCloud Analytics is our data analysis solution for CaseWare Cloud. Analytics enables you to run over 100 tests on your client's financial and business data in order to produce meaningful, detailed reports. Analytics tests look for errors and irregularities in your file, locate duplicates, assess risk, ensure that clients adhere to standards, and more. You can also apply filters and alter the parameters on these tests to suit your requirements. Some Analytics reports are available in chart form, and all reports can be exported for storage in Cloud, on your computer, or directly into your engagement files.

You can upload client data to Analytics from a variety of accounting packages, either by importing them directly to Cloud or by converting them with our Transformer tool. Analytics leverages Big Data technology to process even very large files (several gigabytes in size) in seconds. The tests themselves are easy to run and do not require specialized staff.
