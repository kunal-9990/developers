Engagements page

The Engagements page is the ideal place to start your workday because you can easily review engagement progress and open your engagements from here. You can access the Engagements page from the Cloud menu.

On this page, you can see a list of all the engagements for your organization that you have permission to view. This includes Working Papers files, CaseWareCloud SE (Smart Engagement) engagements, and other supported engagement types. You can see the engagements assigned to you by selecting My Work, making it easy for you to focus on your daily tasks and plan your work schedule.

If you are managing engagements for the organization, you can filter the list of engagements by type, and you can see the status for all of your engagements at a glance. You can even see which milestones were missed by selecting Overdue.

You can select any engagement from the list to view more details. Depending on the engagement type, you can review key dates, outstanding issues and the number of staff hours and total amount billed for the engagement. All of this information is displayed in one place, making it easy to monitor the progress of each of your engagements.
