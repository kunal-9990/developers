Settings

Email settings enable you to set the following information for system-generated emails:

  • The Display Name used in organizational emails

  • The length of time to display in the organization's Email Log

  • Whether or not you want to send detailed notification emails to staff or contacts.

Email Log

The Email Log displays a searchable list of sent emails for review. To view it, go to Settings | Email | Email Log.

You can select any email to view detailed information about it. Emails that were not successfully delivered are marked with a icon.

The time span displayed is set in Email Settings.
