CaseWareCloud Time

CaseWareCloud Time enables accounting teams to plan and budget their time effectively, creating value for clients. Not just a time tracking and invoicing product, Time provides accountants with historical productivity insights to plan for new engagements and streamline the client billing process.

CaseWareCloud Time leverages data from CaseWare Cloud to enable you to generate accurate reports of work performed by staff. Time enables organizations to sync with external calendars to ensure that work in meetings or off-site locations is captured and billed efficiently. Billing is straightforward thanks to our invoice wizard, and organizations can use our customization tools to create and reuse invoice templates to meet their branding and billing needs.

For more information about CaseWareCloud Time, see Setting up Time in Cloud and Setting up client billing.
